Cryptbot

AI-powered cryptocurrency trading based on Swing Trading

How It Works

This trading bot analyzes cryptocurrency market data using technical indicators and a neural network to make automated trading decisions.

  • Fetches historical and real-time market data
  • Applies technical indicators to detect trading opportunities
  • Uses a trained neural network to classify buy/sell signals
  • Executes trades based on risk management rules

Technical Indicators

The bot uses the following indicators to identify potential trades:

  • Exponential Moving Averages (EMA 10, EMA 55, EMA 200)
  • Relative Strength Index (RSI)
  • Average Directional Index (ADX)
  • Squeeze Momentum Indicator (Lazy Bear version)
  • Change Ratio for trend analysis

Neural Network

The neural network is trained to classify market conditions and generate trading signals.

  • Trained on historical price data and technical indicators
  • Uses a deep learning model with multiple layers
  • Predicts probabilities of market conditions for buy/sell signals
  • Continuously updates based on real-time market feedback
